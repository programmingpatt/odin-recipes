Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2.25 teaspoons active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Place water, sugar, salt, oil, bread flour, and yeast into the pan of the bread machine (or in the order recommended by your bread machine manufacturer).
  2. Bake on White Bread setting. Cool on wire racks before slicing.
